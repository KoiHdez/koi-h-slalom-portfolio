UX/UI Design · Product Design

Elevating Design Efficiency: Building a Unified Component Ecosystem

Client: A leading U.S.-based financial services institution

"I'm using Form Builder and it's amazing! It's saving me so much time to create a flow that I can't even describe how happy I am that this is built. I love it!"

Designer — Financial Services engagement

View case study →

Why

Designers across multiple teams lacked a centralized, reliable resource for building new experiences — causing inefficiencies and inconsistencies. The client needed a scalable design system as both a creative launchpad and a single source of truth.

What

Led the creation of a global Figma library shared across three teams, consolidating components, patterns, and templates. Introduced Figma variables for content switching and branching for version control — enabling rapid prototyping at scale.

Wow

Cut time-to-design in half through innovative components like Form Builder. The system became a trusted reference for designers, developers, and PMs. Delivered a multi-library architecture of five interconnected libraries serving distinct purposes.
